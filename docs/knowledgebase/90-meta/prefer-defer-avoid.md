[SYNTH] Guidance for operators and future doc authors extending this knowledgebase.

## Prefer

- [SYNTH] Opt-in channel lists for export and explicit staff/community consent for archival.
- [SYNTH] Evidence labels on every substantive claim; link to repo paths or official docs.
- [SYNTH] Text-only Discord export defaults (`--no-assets`, `--no-reaction-users`) until media policy exists.
- [SYNTH] Chunked `docs/knowledgebase` files over monolithic README dumps.

## Defer

- [SYNTH] pgvector and deep semantic reranking until chunk quality and authority rules are stable.
- [SYNTH] Full attachment OCR and embed-image indexing.
- [SYNTH] Automated reconciliation of Discord edits/deletes beyond periodic re-export.

## Avoid

- [SYNTH] Treating Discord banter as canonical modding fact without corroboration.
- [SYNTH] Ingesting private archived threads or non–bot-visible channels by default.
- [SYNTH] Unlabeled synthesis presented as `[REPO]` or `[OFFICIAL]`.
